Welcome to the TANDY--12!

The TANDY--12 COMPUTERIZED ARCADE is a vintage electronic toy created by the Tandy Corporation. It featured 12 games, which could be played alone, against another player, or against the computer. This recreation allows you to experience what playing the TANDY--12 was like. Although it is an approximate recreation, certain features have been added or changed to ease operation of the unit. In addition, some of the tunes played by the device may be different from the original.

To begin experiencing the TANDY-12 for yourself, tick the ON-OFF checkbox.
