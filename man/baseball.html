Game 7: Baseball

One or two players can play. Standard baseball rules apply. Press START/PITCH-7 to pitch a ball. Flashing lights will move on the TANDY--12; try to hit the ball by pressing PLAY-2/HIT-7 button. A button will flash and tones will sound, indicating the result of your at-bat. Your score will be tracked on the playing board below.
