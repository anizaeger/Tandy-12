Setting up the TANDY--12

Follow these simple procedures to operate your TANDY--12 Computerized Arcade:

  1. Press SELECT button to advance selection until
    desired game flashes. Alternatively, simply tap your
    desired game. During play, press SELECT again to stop
    game play.
  2. Press START to start (or re-start) a game. A beep-
    tone will sound and the selected game number button will
    start to flash.
  3. Proceed to play the selected game as described.
